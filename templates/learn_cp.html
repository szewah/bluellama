HOW TO GET THERE

Central park is located in the heart of New York City, Manhattan between the Upper West Side and Upper East Side, roughly bounded by Fifth Avenue on the east, Central Park West (Eighth Avenue) on the west, Central Park South (59th Street) on the south, and Central Park North (110th Street) on the north making it reachable on foot from many Manhattan neighborhoods. We’re easily accessible via public transportation, all you have to do is hop off the subway at Columbus Circle or Fifth Avenue to enter the park from the south. Local airports include Newark International Airport, LaGuardia Airport and JFK International Airport.
