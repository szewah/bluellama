GRAND CENTRAL NIGHTS

Sleep under the stars of one of New York's most majestic buildings. An extraordinary amalgam of technological sophistication and architectural wonder, Grand Central is a surprising oasis in the heart of Manhattan.

NORDIC DINING EXPERIENCE

DINNER

Agern, meaning “Acorn” in Danish, is a season-driven restaurant and bar, located at Grand Central Terminal, tucked away between Vanderbilt Hall and the 42nd street southwestern passageway entrance.

The menu, from the hands of Icelandic head chef Gunnar Gíslason and his team, features farmed and wild ingredients from the New York State region exposed to time-honored techniques, and flavors rooted in Nordic Cuisine.

Gíslason and his team will be serving you and other guests a ten course meal, curated for the occasion, at the heart of the campsite in the main concourse.

HOW TO GET THERE

Grand Central Terminal is located at 89 E. 42nd Street at Park Avenue and is accessible by subway, bus, train and airport shuttle. There are a number of public parking garages located close to the Terminal.
